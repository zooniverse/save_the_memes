Our Story

Right now Snapshot Serengeti provides the internet with a stead flow of awesome animal photos. Yo are then able to insert those photos into a meme generator, share them on the interweb, and make your friends chucke silently in their cubicles. BUT what would happen if the Snapshot team couldn't afford to operate?

What Can You Do to Help?

Check these awesome perks!

Get any of this awesomeness with your contribution!

  • Spice Up Your Screen

    In addition to the warm, fuzzy feeling that you'll get by donating to Save Snapshot Serengeti we'll also email you 5 awesome high-resolution images from our collection.

  • First Look

    Have your eyes be the first to see photos from Season 7. Funders at this level will get access to Season 7 for an entire week before we open it up to the rest of the world.

  • Snapshot Post Card

    We'll take our favorite Snapshot Serengeti photo, turn it into a postcard, slap on a Tanzanian stamp, and send it to you from our Serengeti research station.

  • Snapshot Greeting Cards

    We'll send you a set of 4 quality cardstock greeting cards. Each will feature a different fantastic photo and be guaranteed dust-free so you can mail them out to friends and family.

  • Wildebeest on Your Wall

    Think the last thing you want to see is *another* wildebeest? How about seeing all the wildebeest at once? A $99 donation gets you a wildebeest photo-mosaic poster to hang proudly on your wall.

  • Serengeti Self-portrait

    Tired of the same old selfie for your Facebook profile? Add a lion! Send us a photo of yourself and we'll bring it into the field and snap a picture of your picture next to our lions.

  • Fancy Photograph

    Our camera traps aren't the only photographers in Serengeti. Our resident lion-tracker, Daniel Rosengren, has generously donated one of his world-class photos to our cause. For a $360 donation, receive this limited edition, gallery quality print, signed by the Snapshot team.

  • Sponsor-a-Camera-Trap

    Your generous donation will not only purchase a new camera trap and theoretically-hyena-proof-case, but also the batteries and SD cards to keep this camera snapping away for years to come.

  • Choose-Your-Own-Adventure-Cam

    Want to sponsor a camera trap but are really tired of Thomson's gazelles? We'll let you *choose* the camera you want to sponsor from your favorite photograph.

  • Dead Camera Centerpiece

    Impress your guests with a mauled camera trap straight from the Serengeti. We lose somewhere between 30-50 cameras a year to hyenas, elephants, baboons, fire, rain, and ants. We'll send you one of these dead traps along with the last picture it ever took.

  • In-person Presentation

    A member of the Snapshot Serengeti team will give a presentation at an event of your choice near Washington DC, Minneapolis, Chicago, Oxford (England), or in the Serengeti itself.

  • Sponsor a GPS collar

    Think radio-collared lions are cool? GPS collars take it to a whole new level. Your donation will get you exclusive access to the live data coming in from our one-and-only GPS-collared Serengeti lion. Plus everything else.
