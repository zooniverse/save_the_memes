Our Story

Right now Snapshot Serengeti provides the internet with a steady flow of awesome animal photos. You are then able to insert those photos into a meme generator, share them on the interweb, and make your friends chuckle silently in their cubicles. BUT what would happen if the Snapshot team couldn't afford to operate?

What Can You Do to Help?

The best method? Head over to the Snapshot Serengeti indiegogo campaign and contribute what you can! Anything you can give will help out the team, even just a buck. Give enough and you'll receive some sweet perks!

What else? Make some memes and help us spread the word! Use our non-patented Meme Generator 5000 and get your chuckle on!

Snapshot Serengeti Needs Your Help!

We've been incredibly grateful for the time you've put into the site over the last few months. It's helped us get science done, and we still want your classifications and help in exploring. But if you could share the campaign link - igg.me/at/serengeti - with your friends and family, that would be great. We're not asking for luxuries - have you seen our drop-toilet? - we're just trying to keep the land rovers chugging along and the cameras clicking by raising money to pay for diesel, equipment, and field assistant salaries.
